About The Digital Den

The Digital Den is more than just a VRChat group — it's a safe haven in the virtual world. Founded on the principles of safety, respect, and community, we've built a space where everyone can feel welcome and protected.


Our structured staff system ensures clear rules and accountability, so you can focus on what matters: having fun, meeting new people, and enjoying everything VRChat has to offer. // Our Guidelines

Community Rules

01 Age Verification ▼

This is an 18+ community. Age verification may be required to access certain areas or participate in events. Please be prepared to verify your age if requested.

02 No NSFW Content ▼

Keep all interactions and content appropriate. This includes avatars, language, and behavior. We are a strictly 18+ community, but NSFW content is not allowed in public spaces.

03 Respect All Members ▼

Treat everyone with kindness and respect. Harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.

04 No Spamming or Trolling ▼

Avoid spamming, trolling, or any disruptive behavior that may affect others' enjoyment of the space.

05 Event Conduct ▼

During events or group activities, refrain from using soundboards or voice changers unless explicitly allowed. This ensures everyone can enjoy the event without distractions.

06 Use Appropriate Avatars ▼

Please use avatars that are respectful and not overly provocative. Avatars that are offensive, overly revealing, or disruptive may lead to a warning or removal.

07 Follow Staff Instructions ▼

Our staff members are here to maintain a safe environment. Please follow their guidance and cooperate if asked to adjust your behavior or avatar.

08 No Advertising or Self-Promotion ▼

Avoid promoting your own events, groups, or social media channels without permission from the ownership team.

09 Reporting Issues ▼

If you encounter any problems or see someone breaking the rules, please report it to our staff. You can do so via our Discord server's support channels or in-world moderation requests.
